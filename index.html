Sinhala-Dictionary by buddhika

An open office dictionary and a corpus for Sinhala, currently holding 83,000+ unique words.

Dependencies

Open Office

Install

You can use the dictionary with Open Office (version >= 3) and by selecting language as Sinhala in Tools ->
